Transportation Services Mobile App Development

In order to ensure a pleasant experience using transportation services, hours of time and effort must go into developing an intuitive and functional mobile app. The three main pioneers of e-scooter and bike transportation services are Bird, Spin, and Limebike. All transportation service mobile apps have the same basic model that includes activation, gps tracking, in-app payment, and deactivation. However, each one of these services has its own mobile app that works a little differently depending on the company and how its service functions.

For example, Bird, has created its own mobile app that is specifically designed to make renting an e-scooter easy and convenient with a tap of the finger and barcode scan. The Bird e-scooter service mobile app processing system consists of seven steps, which include how to find and unlock, start, foot placement, where to ride, brakes, parking, and how to end the ride. These steps are relatively the same for alternative e-scooter and bike services.

Inspired by these preexisting electric transport apps, many startups in this industry are using the same basic model to create their own versions. The essential elements needed to make a successful and usable e-transport mobile app include a way to locate and activate the vehicle, barcode scanning feature, safe security and locking system, and safety guide. In addition, mobile app developers must take into consideration how to create an organized and specific location tracking system and evade urban traffic.

As far as design elements for these mobile apps, many companies are leaning toward a clean and minimal user interface.
