Transportation Services Mobile App Development

In order to ensure a pleasant experience using transportation services, hours of time and effort must go into developing an intuitive and functional mobile app. The three main pioneers of e-scooter and bike transportation services are Bird, Spin, and Limebike. All transportation service mobile apps have the same basic model that includes activation, gps tracking, in-app payment, and deactivation. However, each one of these services has its own mobile app that works a little differently depending on the company and how it functions.

For example, Bird, has created its own mobile app that is specifically designed to make renting a scooter easy and convenient with a scan of a barcode. The Bird onboarding process consists of seven steps, which include how to find and unlock, start, foot placement, where to ride, brakes, parking, and ending the ride.
