A New Era of Electric Transportation

In the past few years, commuters have transitioned over to electric transportation services. Ride-sharing giants, like Uber and Lyft, have answered this high demand by creating their own bike and scooter rental services. Not far behind, startups like Bird and Spin have also joined the game hoping to dominate the industry and are working to improve urban sustainability. Read More

Transportation Services Mobile App Development

In order to ensure a pleasant experience using transportation services, hours of time and effort must go into developing an intuitive and functional mobile app. The three main pioneers of e-scooter and bike transportation services are Bird, Spin, and Limebike. All transportation service mobile apps have the same basic model that includes activation, gps tracking, in-app payment, and deactivation. However, each one of these services has its own mobile app that works a little differently depending on the company and how its service functions. Read More
