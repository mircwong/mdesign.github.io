New Era of Electric Transportation

In the past few years, commuters have transitioned over to electric transportation services. Ride-sharing giants, like Uber and Lyft, have answered this high demand by creating their own bike and scooter rental services. Not far behind, startups like Bird and Spin have also joined the game hoping to dominate the industry and are working to improve urban sustainability. Through mobile app development, these companies are establishing themselves as primary electric transportation services. Without even lifting a muscle, commuters can easily open the company's app and rent a bike or scooter, get to where they need to be, tap a few buttons on their smartphones, and be on their way.
